35  * voxels - A pointer to the raw Vec3data. char[0] & 1 is the origin.
36  * The first row continues for xSize bits. There are zSize rows of Vec3bits,
37  * each xSize bits long. This makes up one sheet. There are ySize sheets.
38  * There may or may not be problems when xSize isn't a power of eight.
39  */
